Research Projects

SolverM: is an accurate and fast Solver for Maxwell's equations applied to nanostructures.

  • SolverM can be accelerated by GPU for ultrafast simulations. In my initial demonstrations, SolverM is 1000 times faster than commercial software and 100-400 times faster than the latest articles in forward simulations of nanostructures.

  • Furthermore, SolverM is programmed with graph attributes, facilitating the rapid computation of numerical gradients. Demonstrations have shown that SolverM can achieve accurate solutions for iterative optimization through hundreds of simulations of practical multilayers in less than 30 seconds.

RCWA simulation: is an efficient Rigorous Coupled-Wave Analysis (RCWA) for simulating sub-wavelength gratings

It has been developed for a wide range of applications: spectroscopic ellipsometry, plasmonic biosensors, meta-atom phase control, plasmonic lithography, nanostructural color..., and offers tutorials replicating published articles
